*Template for how to write a Door file*

PositionX:[X-coordinate]
PositionY:[Y-coordinate}
Name:[Door name]
PartnerDoorName:[Destination door name]
Image:[Image filepath]
Scale:[Scale float. Use , instead of .]
Collidable:[true or false]
Foreground:[true or false]
Observation:[Text file path]

*Remember to set to "Copy Always"*
